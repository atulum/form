Memory Sharing Agreement

Your privacy, security, and consent matter

1. Introduction

This agreement establishes the terms and conditions under which holographic memories are shared using the Echoris platform. It aims to ensure that both the User and the Recipient understand their rights, responsibilities, and limitations when it comes to accessing and interacting with the shared content. This mutual understanding fosters trust, security, and the seamless sharing of emotional and impactful memories.

2. Permissions

The Recipient is granted limited access to specific holographic memories shared by the User. This access includes the ability to view and interact with these memories, such as providing Feelbacks or comments, as permitted by the User. The User retains full control over permissions and has the right to revoke access at any time without prior notice. Any attempt to bypass these permissions is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate suspension of access.

3. Privacy

All holographic memories shared on the Echoris platform are encrypted to ensure privacy and security. The Recipient is prohibited from copying, redistributing, or publicly displaying any shared content without the explicit consent of the User. By agreeing to these terms, the Recipient acknowledges the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of shared moments and agrees to uphold the User’s privacy rights at all times.

4. Disputes

In the event of any disagreements, such as unauthorized use of memories or privacy breaches, both parties agree to resolve the matter through mediation facilitated by Echoris. If a resolution cannot be reached, further action may involve arbitration or legal proceedings in accordance with the platform’s policies and local laws. The User and the Recipient also agree to notify Echoris immediately in case of disputes to ensure timely intervention.

5. Termination

This agreement remains in effect until terminated by either party. Termination can be initiated by providing written notice through the platform or using the designated termination feature in the Echoris app. Upon termination, all access to shared memories is revoked, and the Recipient must delete any locally stored content to ensure compliance with the platform’s privacy standards.
